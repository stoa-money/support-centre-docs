## Canva

### Pot Info

[**Canva Pro**](https://www.canva.com/pro/) gives you access to premium templates, brand kits, AI design tools, and advanced features to create professional-quality designs for work, social media, and marketing.

This Perk entitles you to 12 months access of Canva Pro by depositing the required amount into your Stoa account.

Your deposit is held securely for 12 months.\
Thirty days before your Pot is due to mature, you’ll have two options:

* **Renew** for another 12-month term (you may need to adjust your deposit amount based on the latest Canva Pro pricing), or
* **Withdraw** your deposit once the 12-month term has elapsed.

## Adobe Creative Cloud

### Pot Info

[**Adobe Creative Cloud**](https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud.html) gives you access to industry-leading creative tools including Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and more — everything you need for design, video, photography, and content creation.

With this Pot, you can unlock an **Adobe Creative Cloud Pro subscription** by depositing the required amount into your Stoa account.\
Your deposit unlocks an Adobe Creative Cloud plan for 12 months, with access provided via an activation key that can be redeemed directly with Adobe.

Your deposit is held securely for 12 months.\
Thirty days before your Pot is due to mature, you’ll have two options:

* **Renew** for another 12-month term (you may need to adjust your deposit amount based on the latest Perk value), or
