# Digital Security

## NordVPN

Please see information from the NordVPN Support Centre [here](https://support.nordvpn.com/hc/en-us/articles/19685995851665-How-to-get-NordVPN-with-activation-code).

1. Go to [https://my.nordaccount.com/activate](https://my.nordaccount.com/activate)
2. Follow the on-screen instructions.

## ExpressVPN

Please refer to the ExpressVPN Gift Card FAQs [here](https://www.expressvpn.com/support/manage-account/gift-card-faqs).

#### If you are new to ExpressVPN

1. Go to [expressvpn.com/redeem](https://expressvpn.com/redeem) and enter your redemption code in the redemption code field.
2. Follow the instructions to redeem your subscription.

#### If you are an existing ExpressVPN subscriber

1. Sign in to your ExpressVPN account.
2. Select **Redeem Code** from the side menu and follow the instructions to redeem your subscription. Your redeemed months will be automatically added to the end of your current subscription.
