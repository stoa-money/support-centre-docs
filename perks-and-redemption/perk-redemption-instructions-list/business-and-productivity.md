# Business & Productivity

## Canva

1. Open your browser and go to: **https://www.canva.com**
2. **Log in to your Canva account**
   * If you don’t have one, click **Sign up** and create a free account.
3. After logging in, go to the redemption page: **https://www.canva.com/redeem**
4. Enter your **Canva Pro activation code** into the field provided.
5. Click **Redeem / Apply Code**.
6. Once successful, your account will be upgraded to: ✅ **Canva Pro (1 Year)**

## **Adobe Creative Cloud**

### Redemption Instructions

#### **For New Adobe Creative Cloud Users**

1. Visit [https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud](https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud) and click **Buy now** or **Start free trial**.
2. Create a new Adobe account using your email address, or sign in with an existing Adobe ID.
3. When prompted to enter a payment method, select **Redeem a code** or **Have a prepaid code?**.
4. Enter the **activation key** provided by Stoa and follow the on-screen instructions to activate your **Adobe Creative Cloud Pro** subscription.
5. Once activated, your subscription will be applied to your Adobe account and you’ll have full access to Creative Cloud Pro for the subscription period.

#### **For Existing Adobe Creative Cloud Users**

1. Sign in to your Adobe account at [https://account.adobe.com](https://account.adobe.com).
2. Go to **Plans** → **Manage plan**.
3. Select **Redeem code** or **Add prepaid code** (wording may vary by region).
4. Enter the **activation key** provided by Stoa and confirm.
5. The activation key will be applied to your account, extending or replacing your existing **Adobe Creative Cloud Pro** subscription according to Adobe’s terms.

> **Important:**
>
> * Activation keys must be redeemed directly with Adobe and are subject to Adobe’s terms and conditions.
> * If you already have an active paid subscription, Adobe may apply the activation key once your current billing period ends or convert your plan to prepaid.
> * For account-specific questions or issues during redemption, please contact Adobe Support.
